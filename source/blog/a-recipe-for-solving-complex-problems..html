A Recipe for Solving Complex Problems.

Describe the Problem.

Define Why it's Worth Solving.

Describe the Desired Outcome.

Identify the Obstacles that are Preventing that Outcome from Happening.

Prioritize Them.

Start Working Through Them.

Validate Assumptions Along the Way/The Problem You're Solving May/May Not Be the Right Problem.
